## 2. Safety Information

### General Safety Warnings
- Operate the mower only outdoors and in well‑ventilated areas.
- Only trained adults should operate this machine.
- Keep children, pets, and bystanders away from the mowing area.
- Never carry passengers.
- Do not operate on slopes exceeding **10°**.

### Diesel Engine Safety
- Use **diesel fuel only** – never use petrol.
- Diesel fuel is combustible; handle with care.
- Refuel only when the engine is switched off and cool.
- Avoid inhaling exhaust fumes; never operate in enclosed spaces.

### Driving and Collection Safety
- Always sit in the operator’s seat when starting or driving.
- Never reach into the cutting deck, chute, or collector while the engine is running.
- Disengage blades and stop the engine before emptying the collector.
- Engage the parking brake before leaving the seat.

## 6. Operating Instructions

### Starting the Diesel Engine
1. Sit in the operator’s seat.
2. Engage the parking brake.
3. Ensure blades are disengaged.
4. Turn ignition key to the **ON** position.
5. Wait for the **glow plug indicator** to go out (cold starts).
6. Turn key to **START** until the engine runs.
7. Allow engine to warm up before full load operation.

### Driving the Mower
- Press the forward pedal gradually to move forward.
- Use steering wheel for direction control.
- Stop completely before selecting reverse.
- Reduce speed when turning or operating on uneven ground.

### Engaging the Cutting Blades
1. Set engine speed to medium or high.
2. Engage blade control (PTO).
3. Lower cutting deck to selected height.
4. Mow at a steady pace for effective collection.

### Using the Grass Collection System
- Start mowing with an empty collector.
- Maintain moderate speed for best collection results.
- Empty the collector before it becomes overfull.

### Emptying the Grass Collector
1. Drive to a designated disposal area.
2. Disengage blades and engage parking brake.
3. Operate the collector emptying handle.
4. Return collector to the closed position before resuming mowing.

### Stopping the Mower
- Disengage cutting blades.
- Release drive pedal.
- Engage parking brake.
- Turn ignition key to **OFF** and remove it.

## 7. Cutting Height Adjustment

The cutting height range is **30 mm to 90 mm**.

1. Stop mower and disengage blades.
2. Use the cutting height adjustment lever.
3. Select desired height.
4. Ensure deck locks securely in place.

## 8. Maintenance

### Routine Maintenance
- Clean cutting deck and collector after each use.
- Check engine oil before every mowing session.
- Drain water from diesel fuel filter (if fitted).
- Inspect belts, blades, and chute regularly.

### Blade Maintenance
- Inspect blades every **25 operating hours**.
- Sharpen or replace worn blades.
- Always remove ignition key before servicing.

### Storage
- Clean mower thoroughly before storage.
- Fill diesel tank to prevent condensation or use approved fuel stabiliser.
- Store in a dry, covered location.
- Remove ignition key and disconnect battery (if fitted).

## 9. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Possible Cause | Solution |
