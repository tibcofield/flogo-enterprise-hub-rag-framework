## 5. Before First Use

Before starting the lawnmower:
- Check engine oil level.

## 6. Operating Instructions

### Starting the Engine
1. Place the mower on level ground.
2. Set the throttle control to **START / FAST**.
3. Prime the engine if required (2–3 presses).
4. Pull the recoil starter firmly.
5. Allow the engine to warm up briefly.

### Mowing with Increased Power
- Push the mower at a steady walking pace.
- For tall or dense grass, raise cutting height and mow gradually.
- Let the engine and blade do the work—do not force the mower.

### Stopping the Engine
- Move the throttle control to **STOP**.
- Wait until the blade has fully stopped before moving or cleaning the mower.

---

## 7. Cutting Height Adjustment

The central height adjustment system allows cutting heights from **25 mm to 80 mm**.

1. Stop the engine and disconnect the spark plug lead.
2. Pull the height adjustment lever outward.
3. Select the desired height.
4. Release the lever to lock the setting.

## 8. Maintenance

### Routine Maintenance
- Clean the cutting deck after each use.
- Check engine oil every **5 operating hours**.
- Inspect fasteners and fuel lines regularly.

### Blade and Engine Care
- Sharpen or replace the blade every **25 operating hours**.
- Clean or replace air filter as required.
- Use approved replacement parts only.

### Storage
- Empty fuel tank or add fuel stabiliser before long‑term storage.
- Store in a dry, well‑ventilated location.
- Disconnect spark plug lead during storage.
