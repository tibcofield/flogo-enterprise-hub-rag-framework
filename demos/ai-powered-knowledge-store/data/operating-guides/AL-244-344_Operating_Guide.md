## 6. Operating Instructions

### Powering On the Mower
1. Sit in the operator’s seat.
2. Engage the parking brake.
3. Ensure cutting blades are disengaged.
4. Insert power key or press **START** button.
5. Check battery level on display.

### Driving the Mower
- Press the forward pedal gradually to move forward.
- Use steering wheel to control direction.
- Come to a full stop before selecting reverse.
- Reduce speed when turning or operating on uneven ground.

### Engaging the Cutting Blades
1. Set ground speed to low or medium.
2. Engage the blade activation switch.
3. Lower cutting deck to selected height.
4. Begin mowing at an even pace.

### Using the Grass Collection System
- Start mowing with an empty collector.
- Maintain moderate speed for optimal collection.
- Monitor collector fill level.
- Stop mowing to empty the collector when full.

### Emptying the Grass Collector
1. Drive to the disposal area.
2. Disengage blades and engage parking brake.
3. Operate the collector emptying handle.
4. Lower collector fully before resuming work.

### Powering Off
- Disengage cutting blades.
- Release drive pedals.
- Engage parking brake.
- Turn power key or switch system **OFF**.

### Storage
