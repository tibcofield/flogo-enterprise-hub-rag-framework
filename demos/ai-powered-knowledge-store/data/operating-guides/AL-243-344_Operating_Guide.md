## 5. Before First Use

Before operating the lawnmower:
- Check engine oil level.
- Fill fuel tank with fresh unleaded petrol.
- Fully charge the starter battery.
- Inspect blade and cutting deck.
- Set cutting height appropriate for grass length.

## 6. Operating Instructions

### Starting the Engine (Electric Start)
1. Place the mower on level ground.
2. Set the throttle control to **START / FAST**.
3. Ensure the battery is connected.
4. Press and hold the **START** button until the engine starts.
5. Release the button once the engine is running.

### Starting the Engine (Manual Recoil)
If the battery is discharged:
1. Set throttle to **START / FAST**.
2. Pull the recoil starter firmly until engine starts.

### Mowing
- Push the mower at a steady walking pace.
- For tall or dense grass, raise cutting height and mow in stages.
- Avoid forcing the mower; let the blade speed do the work.

### Stopping the Engine
- Move the throttle control to **STOP**.
- Wait until the blade has fully stopped before handling.

## 7. Cutting Height Adjustment

The central height adjustment system allows cutting heights from **25 mm to 80 mm**.

1. Stop the engine and disconnect the spark plug lead.
2. Pull the height adjustment lever outward.
3. Select desired cutting height.
4. Release the lever to lock the setting.

## 8. Maintenance

### Routine Maintenance
- Clean cutting deck after each use.
- Check engine oil every **5 operating hours**.
- Inspect electrical connections and cables regularly.

### Battery Maintenance
- Recharge the battery after extended use.
- Store battery in a dry place during long‑term storage.
- Do not operate with a damaged battery or cables.

### Blade and Engine Care
- Sharpen or replace blade every **25 operating hours**.
- Clean or replace air filter as required.
- Use approved replacement parts only.

## 9. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Possible Cause | Solution |
