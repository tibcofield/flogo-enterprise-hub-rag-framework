2. Safety Information
General Safety Warnings

Operate the lawnmower only outdoors.
Never allow children or untrained persons to use the machine.
Do not operate while tired or under the influence of alcohol or medication.
Keep hands, feet, and loose clothing away from moving parts.

Fuel Safety

Use unleaded petrol only.
Refuel outdoors with the engine switched off and cooled.
Do not smoke or use open flames near fuel.
Clean up spilled fuel immediately.

Operating Area

Remove stones, sticks, wire, and other debris before mowing.
Never mow on slopes steeper than 15°.
Be cautious when mowing near drop‑offs or edges.

Attach the Handle

Align handle sections and secure using the supplied bolts.
Tighten firmly but do not overtighten.



Install the Grass Collection Bag

Lift the rear discharge flap.
Hook the bag onto the mounting points.
Lower the flap gently onto the bag.



Check Fluids

Ensure engine oil is added before first use.

5. Before First Use
Before starting the lawnmower:

Check engine oil level.
Ensure there is sufficient petrol in the tank.
Inspect the blade for damage.
Check that all fasteners are secure.
Adjust mowing height as required.


6. Operating Instructions
Starting the Engine

Move the lawnmower to a flat surface.
Open the fuel valve (if equipped).
Set the throttle lever to START.
Pull the recoil starter gently until resistance is felt, then pull briskly.
Once the engine starts, allow it to warm up for 30–60 seconds.

Mowing

Push the lawnmower at a steady walking pace.
Overlap each pass slightly for an even cut.
Do not force the mower through tall or wet grass.

Stopping the Engine

Release the throttle control.
Wait until the blade comes to a complete stop before moving or cleaning the mower.


7. Cutting Height Adjustment
The AL‑243‑342 features a central height adjustment system.

Stop the engine and disconnect the spark plug lead.
Pull the adjustment lever outward.
Select the desired height (25 mm to 75 mm).
Release the lever to lock into position.


8. Maintenance
Regular Maintenance

Clean the cutting deck after each use.
Check oil level before every mowing session.
Inspect the blade every 10 hours of use.

Blade Maintenance

Sharpen or replace the blade as needed.
Always wear protective gloves.
Ensure the blade is balanced after sharpening.

Storage

Drain fuel before long‑term storage.
Store in a dry, well‑ventilated area.
Keep out of reach of children.
