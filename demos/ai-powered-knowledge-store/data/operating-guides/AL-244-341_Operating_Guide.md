## 2. Safety Information

### General Safety Warnings
- Operate the mower only outdoors.
- Only trained adults should operate this machine.
- Keep children, pets, and bystanders well clear of the mowing area.
- Never carry passengers on the mower.
- Do not operate on slopes exceeding **10°**.

### Driving and Operating Safety
- Always sit in the operator’s seat when starting or driving.
- Keep hands and feet away from rotating blades and discharge openings.
- Engage the parking brake before starting the engine.
- Stop the engine and remove the key before leaving the mower.

### Fuel Safety
- Use **unleaded petrol only**.
- Refuel outdoors with the engine switched off and cool.
- Do not overfill the fuel tank.
- Clean up any spilled fuel immediately.

## 6. Operating Instructions

### Starting the Engine
1. Sit in the operator’s seat.
2. Engage the parking brake.
3. Ensure blades are disengaged.
4. Set throttle to **CHOKE / START** if cold.
5. Turn ignition key to **START**.
6. Once engine is running, move throttle to operating speed.

### Driving the Mower
- Press the forward pedal gradually to move forward.
- Use the steering wheel to control direction.
- Press reverse pedal only when mower is fully stopped.
- Reduce speed before turning or operating on uneven ground.

### Engaging the Cutting Blades
1. Set engine speed to medium or high.
2. Pull the blade engagement (PTO) control to **ON**.
3. Lower cutting deck to selected height.
4. Begin mowing at a steady pace.

### Stopping the Mower
- Disengage cutting blades.
- Release the drive pedal.
- Engage parking brake.
- Turn ignition key to **OFF** and remove key.

## 7. Cutting Height Adjustment

The cutting height can be adjusted from **30 mm to 90 mm**.

1. Stop mower and disengage blades.
2. Use the cutting height lever.
3. Select desired height position.
4. Ensure the deck locks securely.

## 8. Maintenance

### Routine Maintenance
- Clean cutting deck after each use.
- Check engine oil before every mowing session.
- Inspect belts and blades regularly.
- Check tyre pressure monthly.

### Blade Maintenance
- Inspect blades every **25 operating hours**.
- Sharpen or replace worn blades.
- Always disengage blades and remove ignition key before servicing.

### Storage
- Clean mower thoroughly before storage.
- Drain fuel or use fuel stabiliser for long‑term storage.
- Store in a dry, covered location.
- Remove ignition key and disconnect spark plug lead.

## 9. Troubleshooting
