## 2. Safety Information

### General Safety Warnings
- Operate the mower only outdoors.
- Only trained adults should operate this machine.
- Keep children, pets, and bystanders clear of the operating area.
- Never carry passengers.
- Do not operate on slopes exceeding **10°**.

### Driving and Collection Safety
- Always sit in the operator’s seat when starting or driving.
- Never reach into the grass chute or collector while the engine is running.
- Disengage blades and stop the engine before emptying the collector.
- Always engage the parking brake before leaving the seat.

### Fuel Safety
- Use **unleaded petrol only**.
- Refuel outdoors with the engine off and cooled.
- Do not overfill the fuel tank.
- Clean up fuel spills immediately.

## 6. Operating Instructions

### Starting the Engine
1. Sit in the operator’s seat.
2. Engage the parking brake.
3. Ensure blades are disengaged.
4. Set throttle to **CHOKE / START** if the engine is cold.
5. Turn ignition key to **START**.
6. Once running, move throttle to operating speed.

### Driving the Mower
- Press the forward pedal gradually to move forward.
- Use the steering wheel for direction control.
- Come to a full stop before selecting reverse.
- Reduce speed when turning or on uneven ground.

### Engaging the Cutting Blades
1. Set engine speed to medium or high.
2. Engage the blade control (PTO).
3. Lower cutting deck to selected height.
4. Begin mowing at a steady, controlled speed.

### Using the Grass Collection System
- Ensure the collector is empty before starting.
- Mow at moderate speed for optimal collection.
- Monitor collector fill level.
- Stop mowing and empty the collector when full.

### Emptying the Grass Collector
1. Drive to a suitable disposal location.
2. Disengage blades and engage parking brake.
3. Increase engine speed slightly.
4. Operate the collector emptying handle.
5. Lower the collector before resuming mowing.

### Stopping the Mower
- Disengage blades.
- Release drive pedal.
- Engage parking brake.
- Turn ignition key to **OFF** and remove it.

## 7. Cutting Height Adjustment

The cutting height range is **30 mm to 90 mm**.

1. Stop mower and disengage blades.
2. Use the cutting height adjustment lever.
3. Select desired height.
4. Ensure the deck locks securely in place.

## 8. Maintenance

### Routine Maintenance
- Clean the cutting deck after each use.
- Empty and clean the grass collector regularly.
- Check engine oil before every mowing session.
- Inspect belts, blades, and chute for wear.

### Blade Maintenance
- Inspect blades every **25 operating hours**.
- Sharpen or replace worn blades.
- Always stop engine and remove ignition key before servicing.

### Storage
- Clean mower and collector thoroughly.
- Drain fuel or use stabiliser for long‑term storage.
- Store in a dry, covered location.
- Remove ignition key and disconnect spark plug lead.

## 9. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Possible Cause | Solution |
|-----|---------------|----------|
| Grass not collecting properly | Collector full | Empty collector |
| Poor collection performance | Mowing speed too high | Reduce speed |
| Uneven cutting | Incorrect deck height | Re‑adjust height |
| Engine will not start | Parking brake not engaged | Engage brake |
